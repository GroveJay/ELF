Interface Designer

Lee Sheladon

Position Description

Are you ready to be a part of the cutting edge of virtual reality? We are looking for a student who can help transform the interface for our project, The Lost Manuscript. This is more than a video game. We want to bring the benefits of gaming to the classroom by having Mandarin Chinese students interact with an unfolding narrative that engages and tests their new found knowledge of the language.
The Lost Manuscript utilizes the "cave" at the Rensselaear Tech Park. Giant screens with projected images surround the players and provide a more immersive experience like never before. It is here where you'll be need most.
Research and independent credit is available for participation.

Qualifications

  • Knowledge of interface design. Does not have to be related to video games!
  • Strong documentation skills
  • Must be eager to learn and open to constructive feedback.
  • Colaborative spirit as you work alongside other disciplines to develop fitting interface designs.
  • Knowledge of the Unity game engine is a plus.
